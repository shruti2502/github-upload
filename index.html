Helping Out Unidentified

HOUND leads the fight against abduction, abuse, and exploitation - because every child deserves a safe childhood. The HOUND for Missing & Exploited Children is a private, non-profit organization whose mission is to help find missing children, reduce child sexual exploitation, and prevent child victimization. Helping out families, victims, private industry, law enforcement, and the public to assist with preventing child abductions, recovering missing children, and providing services to deter and combat child sexual exploitation.
